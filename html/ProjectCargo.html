Project Cargo Handling

Explore Our Projects

Our Scope

Pre-Planning and Coordination

  • Conducting a comprehensive analysis of cargo specifications, including weight, dimensions, and unique handling requirements, to develop an optimal project plan.
  • Preparing detailed timelines and contingency plans to address any unforeseen challenges.

Stakeholder Coordination

  • Engaging with all relevant stakeholders, including customs authorities, port authorities, transport operators, crane operators, and other service providers, to streamline and synchronize the project.

Permits, Licenses, and Pre-Shipment Documentation

  • Securing all necessary permits and licenses for oversized or overweight cargo from local authorities.
  • Ensuring accurate and timely preparation of all required customs and port clearance documentation.

Route Surveys and Feasibility Assessments

  • Conducting detailed route surveys in collaboration with transportation partners to identify potential obstacles, road restrictions, and safety concerns.
  • Planning alternative routes if required to ensure smooth and safe delivery of cargo.

Customs Brokerage Services

We specialize in providing end-to-end customs brokerage services, including:

  • Securing special approvals and documentation from government authorities.
  • Preparing Customs Declarations (CUSDECs) and compiling all necessary documents for seamless clearance and forwarding.

Handling Over-Dimensional, Break Bulk, and Heavy Cargo

  • Arranging specialized lifting equipment, such as heavy-duty cranes, to safely handle oversized and heavy cargo in compliance with international safety standards.

Inland Transportation Arrangements

  • Providing specialized vehicles such as multi-axle trailers, low-bed trailers, or modular trailers for safe and efficient transportation.
  • Ensuring secure loading, proper bracing, and balance to protect the cargo during transit.

Discharge and Stacking

  • Deploying specialized equipment for the safe unloading and stacking of cargo at the destination.
  • Facilitating direct delivery to minimize the need for rehandling, particularly for challenging cargo types.

Convoy and Escort Services

  • Arranging for escort vehicles and coordinating with local authorities for police or traffic clearances when handling over-dimensional cargo.
  • Ensuring accurate and timely preparation of all required customs and port clearance documentation.
