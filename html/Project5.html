Efficient Execution of a Major Renewable Energy Project

Cargoserv Completes 5 MW Solar Plant Logistics at Ingiriya


We are pleased to announce the successful completion of the 5 MW Solar Plant Project at Ingiriya. This project showcases our ability to manage complex logistics for large-scale renewable energy infrastructure.

Scope of work handled by Cargoserv:

  • Customs clearance and transport of 18×40' containers to the CLL yard
  • Unloading all containers at the CLL yard using forklift and skilled labor
  • Re-loading onto 56 flatbed trucks and 1 boom truck
  • Transporting all trucks to the Ingiriya Wewila site
  • Unloading at site using a 5-ton boom truck with skilled labor

Our team ensured smooth and efficient logistics execution from port to project site. Every step of the process—from customs brokerage to final unloading—was supervised and carried out by our experienced in-house professionals.

This successful operation highlights Cargoserv’s capability in supporting Sri Lanka’s transition to sustainable energy through reliable and professional logistics solutions.
