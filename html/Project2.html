Advancing Sustainable Logistics:

Cargoserv and Prima Ceylon Forge Strategic Rail Partnership

  • Cargoserv Shipping Ltd. is proud to announce a strategic partnership with Prima Ceylon (Private) Limited to drive forward the Rail Backhauling initiative—an innovative step toward redefining freight movement in Sri Lanka.
  • Under this collaboration, Cargoserv has been appointed the official provider of rail-based Multimodal Transport Solutions for Prima Ceylon's logistical operations.
  • The partnership underscores a shared commitment to promoting sustainable, efficient, and future-ready cargo transport systems.
  • By shifting a significant portion of freight to rail, this initiative enhances operational reliability and contributes to a substantial reduction in carbon emissions.
  • This collaboration encourages environmentally responsible logistics adoption across the corporate sector.
  • It marks a major leap toward greener supply chain infrastructure and sets a benchmark in Sri Lanka’s logistics landscape.
