About Us

We Provide Logistics Services Focused On Quality

Cargoserv is a Ceyline Group company. It was established in 1993 as a shipping Agency Company with a mandate to further develop freight forwarding, cargo consolidations, NVOCC operations, and all related freight operations. It started as a niche market player in almost all the above functions and quickly established itself as a force to reckon with.

Cost-Effective Operation

Our cost-effective solutions ensure that you get the best value for your logistics needs without compromising on quality or reliability.

Wide Range of Equipment

We offer a diverse fleet of modern equipment to handle all types of cargo, ensuring efficient and safe transportation.

Logistics Warehousing

Our state-of-the-art warehousing facilities provide secure and efficient storage solutions tailored to your business requirements.
